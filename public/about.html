research-backed ingredients

  • peptides Active amino acids that visibly plump skin and reduce the look of fine lines.
  • shea butter Hydrates + moisturizes with 5 essential fatty acids (including vitamins E, D, A, and allantoin)
  • niacinamide Nourishes and minimizes the look of pores + improves skin’s overall texture.
  • cupuaçu Helps maintain skin elasticity, improve suppleness for dry, dehydrated skin.
  • hyaluronic acid Supports hydration, locks in water + replenishes moisture.
  • marula oil Lightweight yet rich in fatty acids that help support + soften the skin barrier.
  • squalane Powerful emollient, hydrates + softens skin.
  • açai Antioxidant-rich + defends skin from harmful free radicals.
